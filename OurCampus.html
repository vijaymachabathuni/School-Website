OUR CAMPUS



We are located on a GUNTUR – PONNUR – BAPATLA State highway and 2 KM from National highway (NH-16, Chennai - Kolkata) in a complex of bright buildings in the outskirts of GUNTUR CITY having good access from all areas of Guntur.

For our early year’s pupils we have a kids play zone with a slide, climbing frame and soft surface in the grounds of the school.




BLOCK DIAGRAM OF PRODIGY VIDYALAY CAMPUS



Our high School children have a playing field surrounded by netting for Lawn Tennis clay court, Cricket Pitches, as well as clay courts for Kho - Kho, Volleyball, Khabaddi, shuttle game court, a separate play area for athletics, kids play zone within the boundaries of our School campus.








Inside our buildings we have bright, fully ventilated 500 square feet modern classrooms, each equipped with a green board and LED projectors with dual green and White board in the Computer lab, Library and also in Composite Science lab.

Our library is growing and has been stocked with the most popular youth literature as well carefully selected educationally appropriate books for all age groups.

Pick up and drop off for children is arranged carefully in a one way system. We have adequate parking place for guests and a separate roofed parking place is provided for staff.

There is a robust 24 x 7 surveillance system through CCTV cameras in the entire campus.
