CAREERS AT PRODIGY VIDYALAY

Transcend Educational Society; Budampadu, GUNTUR is presently operating PRODIGY VIDYALAY, Budampadu, GUNTUR. It is an English Medium, Co-Educational School established in the year 2018. The School is presently offering Foundation (Classes Nursery to 2nd), Preparatory (Classes 3-5), Middle (Classes 6–8) and Secondary (Classes 9-10).

ELIGIBILITY

The School is looking for trained qualified and competent faculty, who have an attitude of learning, being loyal to the organization and who can enjoy working with diligence and unwavering commitment. Possessing professional degree (M.Ed/B.Ed/D.Ed & TTP/HTP in case of Telugu and Hindi languages) is mandatory. Proficiency in English is compulsory.

FACILITIES OFFERED

• School provides a safe, conducive, professional environment for employees.

• School organizes a weekly Capacity building Programme for teachers.

• Salary offered is best in the region. Salary is no bar for deserving candidates.

• Free bus facility for staff. Fee concession is offered for staff kids as per the School norms.

Note: Only short listed candidates will be called for a demo Class followed by personal interview.

POSITIONS AVAILABLE

Experienced & Trained (M.Ed/B.Ed) Post Graduates, experienced & Trained (M.Ed/B.Ed/D.Ed) Graduates are required. NTT’s or Montessori Trained Teachers having minimum 02 years of experience can apply.

A Counselor who is M.A in Psychology, an experienced Accountant, a female typist and a receptionist with fluency in English must apply.

Experienced and robustly skilled activity teachers for Music (Vocal & Instrumental), Art & Craft, Classical and semi classical dance teacher and coaches for various games & sports may apply.

Interested candidates are requested to forward their resume to info@prodigyvidyalay.com along with the latest passport size photograph and scanned copies of the necessary documents pertaining to academics, experience and achievements if any. Resume sent through Whattsapp are not accepted.
