ADMISSION CRITERIA AND PROCESS

Admission process for the new session begins from 15th January every year and the academic session usually commences from 1st week of April or 3rd week of June.

Admissions are open for the classes Nursery, LKG (Jr. Montessori) and UKG (Sr. Montessori by direct interaction of the parents with the Admission In-charge. Only limited admissions are open for the students of classes 1 to 10.

All the students seeking admission in class IX and above will have to appear in the screening test in English, Mathematics, Science, Social Sciences, Telugu and Hindi followed by interaction.


AGE LIMIT FOR PRE-PRIMARY CLASSES (AS ON 1ST APRIL OF THE YEAR IN WHICH ADMISSION IS SOUGHT)

• Nursery - 2½ and above

• LKG/Jr. Montessori – 3½ and above

• UKG/Sr. Montessori – 4½ and above

Age limit for the remaining classes will be as per the board norms.


IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Submission of admission form doesn’t imply the grant of admission. The decision of School Management would be final in all matters concerning admission.

Admission will be confirmed only after the submission of necessary documents obtained from the last studied School.

Application form can be obtained from School office between 9.00 A.M – 4.00 P.M.00

Application form can also be downloaded from below, and sent online to www.prodiyvidyalay.com.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

1. Aadhar cards of Student, Mother, father and Guardian.

2. T.C. (Transfer Certificate)/SLC (School Leaving Certificate) in original from the last studied School.

3. A copy of valid date of birth certificate issued by the competent authority in case of admission in Nursery, LKG & UKG.

4. A copy of duly attested Marks statement issued by the last studied School.

5. A copy of caste certificate in case of differently able child, OBC, BC, SC and ST.

6. Latest passport sized photographs of Student (03), Mother, father and Guardian (01 each).
