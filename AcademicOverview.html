OVERVIEW OF ACADEMICS

Prodigy Vidyalay is implementing real world relevant and ingenious Educational practices at par with national and International Education.

Prodigy Vidyalay offers an educational experience for all ages, from 3 to 16, from Nursery to Class 10. Our teaching philosophy, based on the principles of Bloom’s Taxonomy (1956), Meta cognition (Thinking of thinking) (Flavel, 1976) and Multiple Intelligences (Howard Gardner 1983) facilitates and inspires learning for all children and young people.

We believe that children are motivated to learn and develop desire to learn when an educator creates an atmosphere of freedom. We foster culture of thinking in the classroom where the children are given ample opportunities to engage, participate, assist and explore so that they construct new knowledge together.

Within this framework all children learn to believe in themselves and their potential; we show them how to love learning, and how to develop their own minds and personalities. We do this in a warm, inclusive atmosphere with an impressive pupil student ratio that allows us to truly know our children and gives us capacity to address their individual needs.
