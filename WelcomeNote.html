WELCOME TO PRODIGY VIDYALAY HIGH SCHOOL

Prodigy Vidyalay High School is founded in 2018 is run by Transcend Educational Society, Budampadu, Guntur is a young school where our hearts and minds focused on shaping children with holistic and quality education at par with international education and as per the directives of NEP (New Educational Policy). We are a purpose-driven school that aims to create a world leading paradigm of education. As management members we are excited that our school embraces the model of education where we teach children how to learn, how their brains develop and how they develop emotionally. Our school is committed to a student-centered learning approach with the aim that each child is challenged according to their own individual uniqueness.

We believe in constructivist classroom (A thinking classroom with multi dimensional pedagogy), where we help children become inquisitive, participative, reflective and inventive. We also encourage children to be proactive, think logically and critically and be confident to explore and construct new knowledge together in a collaborative learning environment.

Our team of teachers are highly qualified and experienced with adequate competencies and expertise in their own subjects with international mindedness in implementing effective teaching pedagogies in teaching and assessment domains. They have brought their expertise, knowledge and passion to lead, inspire, able to create culture of thinking in the classroom by offering ample opportunities for children to engage, participate so that they construct new knowledge together and help us create an inclusive learning environment where our vision can be realized.

Located beside the St. Mary’s Women’s Engineering College, spread in 1.6 Acres of land in a pleasant , fully ventilated lush green area on the Ponnur Road, Budampadu, Guntur. The school is 2.5 Km from NH-16 and 6.3 Km from the Guntur bus stand, 7.8 Km from the Guntur railway station and 55 Km from the Vijayawada International Airport, Gannavaram. Our school campus offers a sense of community and we love to meet you in person and make you acquaint with our best practices in Scholastic and Co-scholastic domains. We will show our campus and also answer any questions you may have. For more details visit our website www.prodiyvidyalay.com.
