CORRESPONDENT'S DESK

Education by all means is drawing out and exploring the best within the child of his mind and soul. School is a place where the child gets formal education not only to acquire mere knowledge but the place where skills and values are nurtured from early ages (Children of 3-8 years) using the foundation principles of ECCE (Early Childhood Care & Education).

As per the guidelines and foundation principles of ECCE, we adopt a blended teaching pedagogy, where the core practices of Montessori methods and Kinder Garten (KG) methods are used to handle especially the pre primary kids.

I robustly believes that children are motivated to learn and develop desire to learn when an educator creates an atmosphere of freedom. Classroom is a research laboratory where ingenious and effective instructional pedagogies are implemented that suits every childâ€™s interest and pace of learning.

Emphasizing on holistic education, we develop 21st century skills such as Strong communication, collaboration, team work, problem solving, and leadership, creative and critical thinking skills. Inculcation of values in students that builds the character of a person is in the highest priority in our organization.

Child education in School is a family affair. Research proved that more the involvement of parents in children Education more is the success rate of children in their lives. There by strong communication between the School and the parents forms the basis of caring and nurturing of a child.

A Parent, Student & Teacher Meeting is organized on Every 4th Saturday of the month in our School to bridge the gap between the School and home.

The School Education is a tri polar having three important stakeholders namely the Student, the parent and the teacher. I wish all the three stake holders must play their important roles so that the students march on the path of their success to meet their intellectual, social and moral excellence.

Correspondent

Mrs. M. Surya Kumari
