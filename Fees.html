FEE NORMS & MODE OF PAYMENT


1.   Parents are requested to give correct particulars of the student such as Full name, Class and section, admission no. while depositing the fee.

2.   All fees are payable in advance by cash/check of the same date/net banking/phone pay/Google pay in three installments namely 5th July, 5th October and 5th January respectively.

3.   Timings of cash payment – 9.00 A.M to 3.00 P.M only.

4.   In any case the fee must be deposited or transferred by 5th of the defined month, failing which penalty will be charged as per the school norms.

5.   One-time fee payment before 5th July of the running session shall be offered 5% concession.

6.   Parents may kindly keep the cash receipts in safe custody and produce them to the school in proof of their payment for ready reference.

7.   Fees once paid won’t be refunded.

8.   Fee structure is subjected to change.
